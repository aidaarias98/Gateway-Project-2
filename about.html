Our Story

Nonna's is family-owned and run. Josefina Chef and Owner is from Naples, Italy. She started her culinary career when she was just 12 years old working in a bakery in Naples. At 16 she moved to Paris, France where he worked as an apprentice to a vast array of chef's gaining knowledge and experience. Since then Chef Josefina has owned and operated over 12 successful restaurants throughout California and now sharing her talent in North Carolina. Nonna's is a completely scratch kitchen. All food is made fresh and to order. All our sauces are made fresh daily. Especially our old family marinara recipe.
